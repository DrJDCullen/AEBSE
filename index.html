Architectural and Building Services Engineering Tour Spaces

Engineering Annexe

The engineering annexe is used for several aspects of engineering teaching and support.

It covers:

  • In house fabrication
  • Mechanical Engineering teaching equipment
  • Formula Student

Henry Cotton 1st floor suite

The Henry Cotton suite is used by the School of civil engineering and Built Environment for Environmental related student work.

It covers:

  • Science and materials courses (the practical science elements)
  • Building services specific equipment
  • Solar and energy related materials

BRE Exemplar zone

The BRE Exemplar zone is a practical onsite resource that can be used for demonstration and testing of modern construction ideas and services.

It covers:

  • Smartflower Solar system, a sun following solar panel array
  • Exemplar houses, three properties built to the building codes of different eras (1920/30's, 1950/60's, and modern codes)
  • Modular housing constructed offsite

About

This Tour Spaces site is meant to give you an overview of the different locations you may encounter for practical elements of your course of study.

It covers three locations

  • Faculty Engineering laboratories
  • Henry Cotton building Environmental science laboratories
  • Exemplar Houses and Innovation zone
